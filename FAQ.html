  1. What version of the app am I using?

    2. To see what version of the app you are using, go to the “Home Screen”. In the top left-hand corner, tap the “i” to open information. At the bottom of the screen under “Credits” you will see the version number. For optimum performance, be sure this matches the latest version in the app store.

  2. How do I create a new account?

    3. To create new account, install EgyViator app and open the app on your device. On the home screen you will see an empty photo box and the text “You are not signed in” below it. To create a new account tap the photo, on the next page, tap the “Register” button. Enter your information or connect with Facebook. and if you are from a Desktop, you can login by pressing the Login/Register item in the Menu Bar and enter your Registration information.

  3. How do I login to my account?

    4. To login to your account, open the app. On the home screen you will see an empty photo box and the text “You are not signed in” below it. To sign in, simply click the photo and tap the “Sign in” button on the next screen. You can sign in with your email address or with Facebook. and if you are from a Desktop, you can login by pressing the Login/Register item in the Menu Bar and enter your Login information.

  4. How do I delete my account?

    5. We’re sad to see you go! To delete your account please send us a message from Contact Us Page and we’ll take care of the rest.

  5. Help! I’m having some trouble!

    6. Let’s try to fix it! First, is your app up-to-date? Most of the time, problems are easily fixed by simply updating the app. the problem is still exist? you can simply send us the inquirey through the Contact us Page and we'll solve the problem as fast as we can.

  6. How do I sync my account to my devices and EgyViator website?

    7. To sync your account, you must be logged in. Go to the home screen and tap your photo. This will open your account page. At the top of the screen you should see a “Sync” button. Tap the button to sync your account.

  7. Why should I sync my account?

    8. The advantage of syncing your account is that you can view your saved places, saved dates, saved guides and city walks on all your devices and on our webiste.

  8. Where can I see all my saved places?

    9. To see your saved places, Tap the “star list” icon in the top right-hand corner. A list of all your saved places will be displayed.

  9. Do I have to be logged in to save a place?

    10. No, but if you are not logged in, the saved places will only be visible on your current device.
